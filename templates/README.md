# Templates

Policy brief and protocol templates generalized from CWD's production use. Replace all `[bracketed]` placeholders with your organization's details.

## Brief Templates

- `brief_template_transit.md` — Transit and mobility policy briefs
- `brief_template_housing.md` — Housing affordability and displacement prevention
- `brief_template_education.md` — Education equity and chronic absence
- `brief_template_zoning.md` — Zoning, land use, and development

## Protocols

- `rapid_response_protocol.md` — 72-hour production timeline for rapid-turnaround policy analysis

## How to Use

1. Copy the appropriate template for your policy domain
2. Replace all `[bracketed]` placeholders with your data and context
3. Follow the quality standards in the rapid response protocol
4. Every statistic must cite a specific source and year
5. Frame findings as evidence, not advocacy
